About warranty

Our warranty covers all defects in materials or workmanship under normal use for a period of 15 years. This means that if you experience any issues with your renovation during this time, we'll be there to make it right.  If you discover any problems with the materials or workmanship of your renovation project, please contact us immediately. Our team will work hard to resolve any issues and ensure that your home is back to its prime condition as quickly as possible.

Please note that our warranty does not cover any damage caused by improper use or wear and tear, nor does it cover any repairs made by unauthorized personnel.  At American Kitchen&Bath, we're committed to providing our clients with high quality, long-lasting results. Our warranty is a testament to that commitment, and we believe it's one of many reasons why our clients trust us time and time again with their home renovation projects.
