Best Subway Blanco Real Glossy Ceramic Tile (3×6)

If you are looking for inspiration and bathroom ideas, then a bathroom with subway tile is a timeless design choice that stays in trend. Subway tiles were initially featured in the New York Metro because they are clean looking and very quickly kept clean because of the solid and smooth glazed finish. This makes subway tiles ideal for bathrooms as they remain hygienic and resistant to water, wear and tear as much as their appearance. However, compared to other bathroom tiles, subway tiles have an extremely durable appearance and are versatile for bathroom renovation and remodeling. If you want to keep the bathroom style simple with that timeless look, subway tiles are not the kind of tile you need to replace every time new designs come out.
